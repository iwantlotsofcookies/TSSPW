About Us

Hi, we’re the Slightly Scientific team. We’ve always wanted to try and learn about the impressive, innovative, and incredible world around us; ideology to immunology, psychology to pseudology. And of course, our first thought was to create this podcast. On the 1st of every month, we upload a new episode where we come together to laugh, have fun, but most importantly interview these astonishing scientists – we learn so much from them and we hope you’ll do too while listening to the Slightly Scientific Podcast.
